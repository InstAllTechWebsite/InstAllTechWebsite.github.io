Meeting Controls Icons


We will be describing the icon's meaning when in a Microsoft Teams meeting. This is a picture of what the icons look like when in a Teams meeting. We will be describing them from left to right.

  1. The first icon is the People icon. Clicking the People will display everyone in the meeting as well as the controls to invite others.
  2. The next icon is the Chat icon, which will open the text chat on the right side of the window.
  3. Clicking the Hand will raise or lower your hand.
  4. Clicking the Breakout Rooms icon will open the Breakout Rooms setup options.
  5. Clicking the Ellipse will display a list of extra controls.
    1. Clicking Show device settings will display your audio and video devices
    2. Clicking Show meeting notes will display the meeting notes
    3. Clicking Enter full screen will display the meeting in full screen
    4. Clicking Show background effects will show the background options
    5. Clicking Turn on live captions will enable live captions
    6. Clicking Keypad will bring up the keypad
    7. Clicking Start recording will start a recording of the meeting. Remember to hit Stop recording before ending the meeting
    8. Clicking End meeting will end the meeting
    9. Clicking Turn off incoming video will prevent you from seeing other users' videos
  6. Clicking the Camera turns your webcam on or off
  7. Clicking the Microphone turns your microphone on or off
    1. Clicking the Screen Sharing icon will display the screen sharing controls
  8. On the screen sharing menu, you can choose to share one of your screens, one of your windows, a PowerPoint, or a Microsoft Whiteboard
  9. Clicking the Phone will disconnect you from the meeting
