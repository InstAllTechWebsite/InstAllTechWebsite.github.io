How to Watch a Movie


  1. Whatever you device you are on navigate to the netflix app or website
  2. If it asks for your login information enter it in now
  3. Choose your profile if it asks you to choose a profile.If not continue to the next step.
  4. This is the home screen. From here you can look at their curated lists and suggestions to find something to watch. What you are interested in you can just click and go to step 8.
  5. If you have a specific movie in mind press the search icon which looks like a magnifying glass.
  6. Type in what you are looking for and press enter
  7. This will return a set of results and click which one you want to watch.
  8. Now you should be on the page of whatever you chose to watch. Press the play/resume button if it is a movie.
  9. If it is a tv show click through the episodes and choose which one you want to watch and press play.
  10. Congrats you are now watching netflix!
