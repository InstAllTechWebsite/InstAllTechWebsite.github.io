Record a Meeting


  1. When in a Teams meeting find and click the ellipsis (...) button on the toolbar at the top of the screen and choose Start Recording.
  2. The resulting video file will appear in the meeting chat once the recording ends. All other members of the meeting will be notified that the meeting is being recorded.
  3. Congrats you have recorded a meeting!
