How to Share Your Screen


  1. Enter your Teams meeting if you haven't already. If you need instructions on how to join a Teams meeting please use our other tutorial.
  2. At the top of the screen where your meeting controls are located choose the Screen Shareicon outlined in red here:
  3. It will then bring up all the different screens and windows you have open, as well as an option to share a PowerPoint or a Microsoft Whiteboard. Choose the image of the one you want to share.
  4. If you are presenting a video then click the button on the top left of the popup that says Include system audio""".
  5. Press the Sharebutton! Congrats you have successfully shared your computer screen!
