How to Make and Listen to a Groove Playlist


  1. Open the app
  2. Click the big + sign on the left side of the page
  3. Type in a name for your playlist
  4. Click Create Playlist
  5. To add songs, click on the Music icon on the left side of the page to go to My Music
  6. You can add songs individually by moving your cursor over a song and clicking on the +
  7. You can add multiple songs at once by moving your cursor over a song and ticking the box that shows up on the left side of each song. Once all the songs you want to add are selected, right click on a song, choose Add to, and then choose the playlist you want. Another option is to click the + button labeled Add to on the bottom right of the screen, and then select your playlist.
  8. Once all your songs are added, you can listen to your playlist! Click on your desired playlist on the left side of the screen, and then click on the play button to play your music.
