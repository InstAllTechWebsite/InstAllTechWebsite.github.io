How to Install Teams


  1. Go to https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-teams/download-app#desktopAppDownloadregion
  2. Click on the Download Teams""" button
  3. Open the Teams_windows_x64.exe""" file from the downloads list at the bottom left corner of your window to install it on your computer.
  4. If you get any pop-ups requesting permissions from your computer, click Allow.
  5. Wait for the installation to finish. Once it's done, you are ready to use Microsoft Teams!
