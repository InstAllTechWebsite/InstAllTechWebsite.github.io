How to Add a Movie to Your List


  1. Navigate to the netflix app on whatever device you want.
  2. Login if needed and choose which profile you want to watch on.
  3. This is the home screen and you can scroll until you find your current list. This is where you can see whatever has already been added.
  4. You can browse through the home screen and click on any title you are interested in. Go to step 7 now.
  5. You can also use the magnifying glass in the top right of your screen and type in whatever you are looking for.
  6. This will return a list of results. Click on what you want to add to your list
  7. This will then open up the movie or tv shows page. There should be a list of icons under the name of the movie. Look for the icon with a + sign in a circle. Click that icon!
  8. You have successfully added a movie to your list. When on the homescreen of netflix you can scroll to find your list where you can see everything you have added.
