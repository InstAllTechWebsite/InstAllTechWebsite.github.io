How to Make and Listen to a Spotify Playlist


  1. Log in to spotify if you haven't already. These instructions are going to be for making a playlist on the desktop application.
  2. On the bottom left corner of the app there is a button with a circle and plus sign inside that says new playlist next to it. Click it.
  3. Fill in the corresponding information. At the very least give your playlist a name.
  4. Press create playlist! Now it is time to add music to your playlist.
  5. Search in the search bar for music you want to add or click through the ones on display. Chose something that contains music you want to add (a song, album or other playlist)
  6. You should now be looking at the screen of whatever item you clicked on. There should be a list of songs or possibly only one. Hover over the song you want to add.
  7. Now, while hovering there should be a ... towards the end of the line of song information. Move your cursor over to that button and click it.
  8. Click the button add to playlist.
  9. It will then show you a list of your current playlists or press create a new one. Choose which one you want to add to.
  10. Keep adding music following the steps 5-9 until you decide you are done.
  11. Now it is time to listen to a playlist. On the left toolbar there should be a list of your playlist in your library. Click on the one you want to listen to.
  12. You are now on the home screen of the playlist. There is a list of every song in this playlist. You can click the green button that says play to shuffle the playlist or click on individual songs to play!
