How to Create a Spotify Account


  1. On your web browser navigate to www.spotify.com
  2. In the top right corner click the button that says sign up. These instructions will go over how to sign with an email address.
  3. Fill in the designated boxes with the information they are looking for.
  4. Click the recaptcha I am not a robot button at the bottom. This will likely ask you to either type in letters or click certain pictures. Follow the instructions it gives you.
  5. Click the sign up button.
  6. Congrats you have signed up! Now when you go to spotify you can login with the credentials you created today!
