How to Print a Document


  1. Make sure you are in the word file you want to print out.
  2. Click the button in the top left corner that says File.
  3. On the left hand side of the screen press the button that says Print. It should be below Save a Copy and above the Share button.
  4. Choose which printer you want to print to.
  5. Choose other information in the boxes such as copies, double sided, collated etc.
  6. Press the big print button on the top of the screen with a printer icon in it.
  7. Collect your pages from the printer!
