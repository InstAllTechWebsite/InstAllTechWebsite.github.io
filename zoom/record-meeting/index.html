Record a Meeting


  1. When in a zoom meeting find the Record button on the bottom toolbar. It looks like a circle within a circle. Press that button.
  2. Choose if you want to save it on your computer or in the cloud. For this tutorial we will be choosing the computer.
  3. The video will start recording.
  4. When you are done press the Stop Recording button in the top left corner next to where it says Recording. It should be a solid square button. This will end the recording.
  5. Then, a pop up box will appear and ask where you want to save it. Navigate to the folder you want and press OK.
  6. Congrats you have recorded a meeting!
