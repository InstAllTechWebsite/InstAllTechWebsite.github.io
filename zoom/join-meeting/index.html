How to Join a Zoom Meeting


  1. Navigate to the zoom program on your computer. If you do not have the blue icon on your home screen you can search for zoom. You can do this by looking at the bottom bar on your computer where it shows a search bar. Type in zoom and the app will appear. When opening the app it should say Join a meeting or Sign in.
  2. Click the button that says Join Meeting
  3. This is when you will need to navigate to whatever information was shared to you about the meeting you are trying to join. If they gave you a link, press that link and go to step 6.
  4. If the person didn't give you a link and instead gave you a Meeting Id then enter the Meeting Id into the Enter Meeting Id space.
  5. Then enter your name into the name button.
  6. Click Join Meeting
  7. If a button to join with computer audio pops up click the button.You are now in the meeting!

If you want to know more about meeting controls look at our Meeting Controls tutorial!
