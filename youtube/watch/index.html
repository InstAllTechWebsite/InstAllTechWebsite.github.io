How to Watch a Video


  1. Open up the internet browser you want to use. You can do this by looking for the icon on your desktop and double clicking on it.
  2. In the navigation bar at the top of your screen enter in www.youtube.com
  3. Press enter
  4. You are now on the youtube site! You can click on any video already on the screen to watch it or search for your own.
  5. There is a small search bar at the top of the youtube screen where you can type in what you are looking for and press enter.
  6. You will then get a list of results and you just click on the one you want to watch.
  7. It will start playing and you can click the screen again or the little pause button in the left corner to pause.
  8. You can click the square with missing parts icon in the bottom right of the video player to make the video full screen
  9. To then minimize the video you click the icon again in the bottom right corner or press the Escape key on your keyboard.
