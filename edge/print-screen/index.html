How to Print Your Screen


  1. Navigate to what you want to print out.
  2. Press the ... button in the top right corner of the window.
  3. Click the button that says print.
  4. A popup box of information should appear with a preview of your document.
  5. On the left hand side first choose what printer you want to print to or if you want to print to pdf.
  6. Chose if you want to print in color or not
  7. Also how many copies you want to print.
  8. Then check the box if you want to print double sided.
  9. Click the print button and collect your pages from the printer!
